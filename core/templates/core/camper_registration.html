Thank you for your interest in the {{camp.start_date|date:"Y"}} ASPYRE Leadership Camp located at Camp Weaver in Greensboro, NC on {{camp.start_date|date:"l"}}, {{camp.start_date|date:"F"}} {{camp.start_date|date:"dS"}}, {{camp.start_date|date:"Y"}} through {{camp.end_date|date:"l"}}, {{camp.end_date|date:"F"}} {{camp.end_date|date:"dS"}}, {{camp.end_date|date:"Y"}}. The camp is a leadership retreat for LGBTQ youth and allies ages 13-17, or 18 and still in high school. The campers will be creating an action plan for changes they want to see in their community and learning the skills they need to jump start their efforts. They will also have the opportunity to network with other LGBTQ youth from across the state, make new friendships, and get inspired.

Before you register, please note, the camp will cover many sensitive topics that can potentially be triggering. We will attempt to sufficiently warn campers, however the camp is designed to prepare them to lead a movement in their community where they may have to face these topics. Parental permission for all attendees under 18 will be required for this overnight event. If you have questions, please contact athomas@lgbtcenterofraleigh.com. We look forward to having you join us for this great event sponsored by the LGBT Center of Raleigh!

Please note that transportation must be provided to and from the camp as we are not able to provide that service. Once registered, the camper will be invited to the Facebook group specifically for camp attendees. You are welcome to coordinate carpooling if desired. However, please keep in mind that the LGBT Center of Raleigh and the ASPYRE Leaders (youth or adult) are not responsible for coordinating, follow through of transportation, or incidents occurring while carpooling.
