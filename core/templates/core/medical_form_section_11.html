SECTION 11 - Directions To The Camp

DIRECTIONS TO THE CAMP:

ENTRY SIGNS OFF OF MILL POINT ROAD

CASPER HALL

TO THE CAMP SITE:
  • Follow GPS Directions to 4924 Tapawingo Trail, Greensboro, NC 27406
  • From I-40 W/I-85 S: Exit #129, Make a Left onto Young’s Mill Road. (From I-40 E/I-85 N make a right)
  • Follow Young’s Mill Road until it dead ends and make a left onto Mill Point Road.
  • Travel on Mill Point Road for ¾ of a mile and turn onto Tapawingo Trail. Once inside the camp go ¾ around the traffic circle and make a right toward Casper Hall
We will have signage to assist in finding your way to registration. Please call if you need any assistance.
